# Datasets
In the dataset-based evaluation, we collect a dataset of \rev{432} screenshots %directly 
from the AR device during AR system use. The %collected 
images cover 4 scenes (office, living room, bedroom, and café), 5 private information types (ID cards, credit cards, password notes, and text displayed on computers or phones), and 6 virtual objects in AR interactions (coffee cup, whiteboard, indoor plant, guitar, vase, and chair).
